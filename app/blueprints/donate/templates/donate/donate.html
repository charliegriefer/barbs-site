Helping Paws Recurring Donation

Your automatic monthly or quarterly donation provides Barb's Dog Rescue with continuous and reliable resources – and helps us plan our monthly budgets.

Benefits of recurring donations:
  • Provides steady, reliable funding for ongoing care
  • Helps us plan food and medical supplies purchasing
  • Reduces administrative costs so more goes directly to the dogs
  • Automatic processing - set it and forget it!

You'll receive an email confirmation after each donation is processed.

Make an IRA Gift!

If you are 70 1/2 or older, you can make a qualified charitable distribution (QCD) from your IRA to support Barb's Dog Rescue. A QCD is a tax-free donation, and if you are 73 or older, it counts toward your required minimum distribution for the year.

Please provide your plan administrator with the following information to make a qualified charitable distribution from your IRA:
