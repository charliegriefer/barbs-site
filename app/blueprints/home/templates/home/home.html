A Lifeline for Dogs in Need

Since 2001, Barb Mumaugh and her dedicated team have been rescuing dogs from the streets and beaches of Rocky Point, Mexico. What started as helping a few strays has grown into a full-scale rescue operation that has saved thousands of dogs.

Our shelter provides food, medical care, and rehabilitation to dogs that have been abandoned, injured, or born on the streets, giving them a second chance at life and love.

At Barb's Dog Rescue, we're committed to:

  • Rescuing and rehabilitating dogs in desperate situations
  • Providing spay/neuter services to reduce the stray population
  • Finding loving forever homes through our adoption program
  • Educating the community about responsible pet ownership

We operate entirely on donations and rely on the generosity of supporters like you to continue our life-saving work.
